\begin{toolkitbox}{Ordered Sets --- Quick Reference}
\small
\begin{tabular}{l l l}
\toprule
\textbf{Concept} & \textbf{Meaning} & \textbf{Detail} \\
\midrule
Ordered set        & $(A, \leq)$: set with a partial order                        & \hyperref[def:ordered-set]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Strict order       & $a < b \iff a \leq b \land a \neq b$                         & \hyperref[def:strict-order]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Comparable / incomparable & $a \leq b$ or $b \leq a$ / neither              & \hyperref[def:comparable]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Total (linear) order & Partial order with all pairs comparable                  & \hyperref[def:linear-order]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Upper / lower bound & $u \geq s$ / $\ell \leq s$ for all $s \in S$               & \hyperref[def:bounds]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Minimal / maximal  & No smaller/larger element in $S$                            & \hyperref[def:min-max]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Least / greatest   & Smaller/larger than all elements of $S$                     & \hyperref[def:least-greatest]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Order-preserving   & $a \leq b \Rightarrow f(a) \leq' f(b)$                       & \hyperref[def:order-pres]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Order isomorphism  & Bijective order-preserving map with order-preserving inverse & \hyperref[def:order-iso]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Well-ordered set   & Every nonempty subset has a least element                   & \hyperref[def:well-order]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Chain / antichain  & All comparable / none comparable                            & \hyperref[def:chain]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Initial segment    & Downward-closed subset                                      & \hyperref[def:initial-segment]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{toolkitbox}

\begin{toolkitbox}{Lattices --- Quick Reference}
\small
\begin{tabular}{l l l}
\toprule
\textbf{Concept} & \textbf{Meaning} & \textbf{Detail} \\
\midrule
Lattice              & Poset with $\sup\{a,b\}$ and $\inf\{a,b\}$ for all $a,b$   & \hyperref[def:lattice]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Join / Meet          & $a \vee b = \sup\{a,b\}$; $a \wedge b = \inf\{a,b\}$        & \hyperref[def:lattice]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Complete lattice     & Every subset has sup and inf                                & \hyperref[def:complete-lattice]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Distributive lattice & $a \wedge (b \vee c) = (a \wedge b) \vee (a \wedge c)$      & \hyperref[def:distributive-lattice]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
Boolean lattice      & Complemented distributive lattice                           & \hyperref[def:boolean-lattice]{$\downarrow$ Def} \\
$\mathcal{P}(X)$     & Complete Boolean lattice under $\subseteq$                  & \hyperref[ex:power-set-lattice]{$\downarrow$ Ex} \\
$\sigma$-algebras    & Sub-lattices of $\mathcal{P}(X)$ closed under countable ops  & \hyperref[rem:sigma-alg-lattice]{$\downarrow$ Rem} \\
Knaster--Tarski      & Every monotone map on a complete lattice has a fixed point   & \hyperref[thm:knaster-tarski]{$\downarrow$ Thm} \\
