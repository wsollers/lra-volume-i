\section{Semantics}

\begin{toolkitbox}{Toolkit}
This section uses truth assignments, recursive extensions, satisfaction,
truth tables, and logical equivalence to assign meanings to formulas.
\end{toolkitbox}

\begin{remark*}[Exposition]
The semantics section assigns truth conditions to the syntactic formulas
introduced earlier. Truth assignments, recursive extensions, satisfaction,
and truth tables provide the machinery for validity, consequence, equivalence,
and semantic classification.
\end{remark*}
