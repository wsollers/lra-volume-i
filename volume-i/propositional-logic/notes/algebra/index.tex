\section{Algebra of Propositions}

\begin{toolkitbox}{Toolkit}
This section uses logical equivalence, equivalence laws, formula contexts, and
replacement of logical equivalents to treat propositions algebraically.
\end{toolkitbox}

\begin{remark*}[Exposition]
The algebra section treats logically equivalent formulas as interchangeable
for calculation. Equivalence laws and replacement principles turn semantic
agreement under every truth assignment into a controlled rewrite calculus for
propositional formulas.
\end{remark*}
